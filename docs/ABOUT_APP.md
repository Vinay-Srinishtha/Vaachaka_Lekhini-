# About Vaachaka Lekhini

Vaachaka Lekhini is a personal spiritual practice companion for chanting, writing, tracking, and growing a daily mantra discipline. The app helps individuals and families choose meaningful mantras, set practice goals, count daily recitations, and follow their progress over time.

Built for devotion with structure, Vaachaka Lekhini combines traditional practice flows with modern tools like voice-based counting, handwriting capture, family profiles, reward points, and community encouragement.

## What You Can Do

### Choose a Mantra

Browse a curated mantra catalog with names, descriptions, deity information, need-based recommendations, and pronunciation support. You can choose a mantra directly or select one based on your spiritual need, such as peace, healing, protection, devotion, wisdom, strength, or prosperity.

### Train Your Voice

Vaachaka Lekhini can learn your chanting pattern so it can help count repetitions during practice. Voice practice is designed to support hands-free chanting while keeping manual counting available as a fallback.

### Submit Your Handwriting

The app supports multiple handwriting options:

- Write directly on the screen
- Capture handwriting using the camera
- Upload handwriting from the gallery
- Use the app's default font

This makes mantra writing practice flexible, whether you prefer digital writing, photographed notebook pages, or a simple standard-font flow.

### Set Practice Goals

Create mantra programs with a target count and duration. Vaachaka Lekhini calculates daily practice targets and helps you stay consistent across multi-day goals.

### Count Daily Practice

Use the practice counter to track chanting or writing sessions. The app supports voice and manual modes, records completed sessions, and updates your daily progress automatically.

### Track Progress

View your active programs, total chants, daily targets, streaks, milestones, and calendar-based progress. Each session contributes to your long-term practice history.

### Practice With Family

Create family member profiles under one account. Each member can have their own practice counter, programs, progress, and rewards.

### Join the Community

The community area shows streaks, total chants, and invite options so users can share their spiritual journey with friends and family.

### Earn Rewards

Completing sessions and reaching milestones can earn reward points. Points can be viewed in the reward history and redeemed in the reward store.

## Key Features

- Mantra catalog with multi-language support
- Need-based mantra recommendations
- Voice training for chant counting
- Manual counter fallback
- Handwriting submission flows
- Goal-based mantra programs
- Daily progress tracking
- Streak and milestone tracking
- Family member profiles
- Community leaderboard and invites
- Reward points, store, and transaction history
- Settings for language, reminders, voice, privacy, and profile management

## Privacy First

Vaachaka Lekhini is designed to keep personal practice data private. Voice and handwriting flows are treated as sensitive spiritual and personal data. The app is structured so practice data can live locally on the device, with backend sync used only where required for account, community, store, and admin-managed content features.

## App Purpose

Vaachaka Lekhini exists to make spiritual discipline easier to begin, easier to continue, and easier to share with family. It supports users who want a simple, respectful way to chant, write, count, and complete mantra goals with consistency.
