## What does the name mean?

**Vachika** (वाचक) means *speaker, reciter, chanter* — from the Sanskrit root *vāc* (voice, speech). In yogic tradition, vāchika refers to mantra spoken aloud.

**Lekhini** (लेखिनी) means *the writer, the scribe* — feminine form from Sanskrit *likh* (to write). A pen, an instrument of disciplined inscription.

Together, Vachika Lekhini embodies both ancient forms of mantra practice — to chant with your voice and to etch each syllable with your hand.

## Three Ways to Practice

### 🎙️ Voice Chanting
Chant aloud and let the app count your recitations automatically. The microphone listens, AI recognises your mantra, and your tally grows — hands-free, eyes-closed. All processing happens entirely on your device using an offline speech model. Your voice never leaves your phone.

### ✍️ Handwriting
Write the mantra by hand on your screen or upload a photo of your written practice. OCR verifies each inscription. The ancient discipline of likhit japa, now digital. All recognition is done offline on your device.

### 👆 Manual Counter
A simple, elegant digital mala. Tap once per recitation. No permissions needed. Perfect for silent practice, meditation halls, or when your voice is your inner voice.

## Features

**🎯 Goal-Based Sadhana**
Set a target count and number of days. The app calculates your daily target and adjusts it dynamically as you progress — keeping you on pace without pressure.

**🔥 Streaks & Milestones**
Track your current and longest streaks. Reach milestone chant counts — 1 lakh, 5 lakh, 10 lakh — and earn bonus reward points.

**👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Profiles**
Add up to 5 family members under one account. Each member has their own program, streaks, rewards, and practice history.

**🌐 Global Sadhanas**
Join community-wide chanting campaigns. Contribute your counts to a collective global goal alongside thousands of fellow practitioners.

**🏆 Practice Circle**
Invite friends to your personal practice circle. Compare streaks and totals in a private, encouraging leaderboard.

**🔔 Daily Reminders**
Set a reminder at a time that suits your routine. Choose from conch, singing bowl, chime, or silent notification.

**🎵 Sruthi Ambience**
Practice with optional background musical accompaniment — a gentle sruthi drone to anchor your focus.

**📤 Share Your Journey**
Share milestone achievements and daily quotes as beautifully formatted images via WhatsApp, Instagram, or any platform.

**🏪 Rewards Store**
Redeem earned points for spiritual resources: shrine frames, books, meditation tools, and guided sessions.
